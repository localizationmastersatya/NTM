About NTM Translations

We are a language services team in Chennai. Translation is what we do, and we take it seriously.

Who we are

NTM Translations started because we kept seeing translations that were technically correct but sounded wrong. A birth certificate with awkward phrasing. A website that clearly was not written by a native speaker. That is the kind of thing we wanted to fix.

We are based in Chennai, a city where several languages meet every day. That background helps us understand how people actually use language, not just how textbooks say they should.

Whether you need one document translated or regular support for your business, we treat the work the same way: get the meaning right, keep the tone natural, and deliver on time.

  • ✓
    Accuracy We read through every translation before sending it back. Names, numbers, and terms get checked carefully.
  • ✓
    Communication If we have a question about your project, we ask. No guessing and hoping for the best.
  • ✓
    Privacy Your documents are not shared with third parties. We handle sensitive material with care.

How it works

From first email to final file

Nothing complicated. Here is how a typical project goes.

1

You send the details

Tell us what you need translated, the languages involved, and when you need it. We will come back with a price and timeline.

2

We assign the right person

Legal documents go to someone with legal experience. Medical content goes to someone who knows medical terms. It is not one-size-fits-all.

3

Translate and review

The translator finishes the work. A second person reads it through before anything is sent to you.

4

Delivery

You get the files on the agreed date. If something needs a minor adjustment, we handle that too.
