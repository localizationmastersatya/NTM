Answers to questions people usually ask before sending us a project.

Send an email to freelance.linguist22@gmail.com or use the form on our contact page. Include the languages involved, what type of content it is, roughly how long it is, and when you need it. We usually reply with a quote within a day.
Our main work is with European languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Polish, and others. We also cover Indian languages like Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada, plus Arabic, Chinese, and Japanese. If yours is not on the list, just ask.
