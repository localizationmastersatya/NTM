Our Services

Here is what we offer. If your need is not listed, ask anyway. We may still be able to help.

📄

Document Translation

Personal and official documents translated carefully. We keep your formatting where we can and treat sensitive files with discretion.

  • Birth & marriage certificates
  • Legal contracts & affidavits
  • Medical records & reports
  • Academic transcripts & degrees
  • Business & financial documents
  • Immigration & visa paperwork
🌐

Website & App Localization

Translation alone is not enough for websites and apps. The text needs to fit the layout, match local usage, and read naturally. That is what localization is for.

  • Website content & SEO copy
  • Mobile app strings & UI
  • E-commerce product listings
  • Software & SaaS interfaces
  • Marketing & ad campaigns
  • Multilingual content management
🎬

Subtitling & Transcription

Subtitles timed properly for video, or a full transcript of an audio recording. Useful for films, training videos, podcasts, and social media content.

  • Video subtitles (SRT, VTT)
  • Film & documentary dubbing scripts
  • Webinar & podcast transcription
  • Corporate training videos
  • Social media content
  • Live event captioning support
🎙️

Interpretation

When you need someone in the room (or on a call) to translate speech in real time. We cover business meetings, medical visits, legal settings, and events.

  • Business meetings & negotiations
  • Medical & healthcare settings
  • Legal & court proceedings
  • Conferences & seminars
  • Phone & video remote interpreting
  • On-site interpretation in Chennai
✏️

Proofreading & Editing

Got a translation from somewhere else and it does not sound right? Send it to us. We will fix grammar, awkward phrasing, and terminology so it reads properly.

  • Translation quality review
  • Copy editing & proofreading
  • Terminology consistency checks
  • Style guide alignment
  • Multilingual document review
  • Pre-publication final polish
